Greeting

Hello my future boss or potential bosses or nice people who agree to refer me. My name is Wenjing Duan, and I am currently a Master student in Computer Science at Viterbi School of Engineering at University of Southern California. Don't get me wrong, this is not my portfolio, this is a self-driven web application whose contents are all around my daily life. This web app is built from scratch using Express as front-end, Node.js and Mongodb as back-end framework. I will describe more details about the technique details in Tech Diary.

Well, you may wondering, why do you build this site? Especially in today's world, the least things we lack are public platforms where people could show their lives and thinkings to friends or even the world. Do you do this to learn new frameworks and technologies? Do you do this to make your experiences more competitive? Do you do this to promote yourself? The answer is: yes, why not. But besides, I want to create my personal "comfort zone". Inspired by lots of Youtubers, I started to think why not designing my own digital diary so that I could do, post and save all great things I like in my life and make my day. I can I excel at learning new technologies and applying them in right place. So no matter what I want to display, I can manage that.
